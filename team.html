Expanding Our Horizons: Solar, Electrical, and Fire Safety Projects

Discover our active projects, including our venture into solar power plants, electrical substations, and fire safety mechanisms. Each project showcases our expertise and dedication to delivering top-tier solutions in critical infrastructure development.

Solar Power Plants

Diversified Into Developing Solar Power Projects By Undertaking A 125 Crore Contract From NTPC. The Company Is Very Optimistic About This Vertical And After The Commissioning Of The Plant, The Company Is Eligible To Apply All Renewable Energy Tenders.

Electrical Substations

The Company Is Building And Erecting Electrical Submissions On EPC Basis. Safety Controls Is One Of The Few Companies, Who Has The Technical Qualification To Participate In The Largest Substation Tenders In The Country.
